Predicting a Pulsar Star

Pulsars are a rare type of Neutron star that produce radio emission detectable here on Earth.Each pulsar produces different emission pattern. Using the parameters obtained from the emission spectrum was able to train the HTRU2 data set against various standard classification models. HTRU2 is a data set which describes a sample of pulsar candidates collected during the High Time Resolution Universe Survey .The highest accuracy achieved after hyper-parameter tuning was 97.8% (SVM model).
